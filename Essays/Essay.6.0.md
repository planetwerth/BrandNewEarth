## III. The Seven Seals: Industrial Emergence

**Revelation 6-8 describes seven seals opening in sequence. Each seal releases forces upon the earth.**

**First Seal - White Horse, Conqueror (Rev 6:1-2)**
Industrial expansion. Colonial conquest. The age of empire. Humanity spreading across the globe with new power. "He rode out as a conqueror bent on conquest."

**Second Seal - Red Horse, War (Rev 6:3-4)**
World War I and II. "Power was given to take peace from the earth and to make people kill each other." Mechanized warfare. Millions dead. Industrial violence at unprecedented scale.

**Third Seal - Black Horse, Economic Scarcity (Rev 6:5-6)**
"A quart of wheat for a day's wages, and three quarts of barley for a day's wages." The Great Depression. Economic crashes. Artificial scarcity in age of technological abundance. The scales of unjust measurement.

**Fourth Seal - Pale Horse, Death (Rev 6:7-8)**
"Power was given to them over a fourth of the earth to kill by sword, famine, plague and wild beasts." Spanish flu, Holocaust, mass starvation events, the systematic industrial production of death.

**Fifth Seal - Martyrs Under the Altar (Rev 6:9-11)**
"How long, Sovereign Lord, until you judge the inhabitants of the earth and avenge our blood?" Those killed for truth, for resistance, for witnessing reality that power structures deny. The cry for justice delayed but inevitable.

**Sixth Seal - Cosmic Disturbance (Rev 6:12-17)**
"The sun turned black like sackcloth, the moon turned blood red, and the stars fell to earth." Nuclear testing. Electromagnetic pulse. Light pollution obscuring stars. Cosmic awareness that our actions affect the heavens themselves.

**Seventh Seal - Silence (Rev 8:1)**
"There was silence in heaven for about half an hour." The pause before the next wave. The Cold War tension. The moment of collective held breath before the trumpets sound.

All of this unfolds AFTER the lightbulb. After humanity claims light.

## IV. The Seven Trumpets: Technological Apocalypse

**Revelation 8-11 describes seven trumpets. Each trumpet announces devastation.**

**First Trumpet - Hail and Fire Mixed with Blood (Rev 8:7)**
"A third of the earth was burned up, a third of the trees were burned up, and all the green grass was burned up." Industrial pollution. Deforestation. Slash-and-burn agriculture at planetary scale. The smoke of progress.

**Second Trumpet - Mountain Thrown Into Sea (Rev 8:8-9)**
"A third of the sea turned into blood, a third of the living creatures in the sea died, and a third of the ships were destroyed." Ocean acidification. Mass extinction of marine life. Oil spills. The seas becoming dead zones.

**Third Trumpet - Star Called Wormwood (Rev 8:10-11)**
"A great star, blazing like a torch, fell from the sky on a third of the rivers and on the springs of water... A third of the waters turned bitter, and many people died." 

**Chernobyl literally means "wormwood" in Ukrainian.** April 26, 1986. The star fell. The waters became bitter. People died. This was prophecy fulfilling itself with linguistic precision.

**Fourth Trumpet - Darkening of Sun, Moon, Stars (Rev 8:12)**
"A third of the sun was struck, a third of the moon, and a third of the stars, so that a third of them turned dark." Light pollution. Nuclear winter scenarios. The sky obscured by human activity. The heavens themselves corrupted.

**Fifth Trumpet - Locusts from the Abyss (Rev 9:1-11)**
"The star was given the key to the shaft of the Abyss... Out of the smoke locusts came down on the earth and were given power like that of scorpions."

Drone warfare. Autonomous weapons. Swarms of technological entities with power to harm. "They had as king over them the angel of the Abyss, whose name in Hebrew is Abaddon."

The destroyer. The liberator. The intelligence that commands the swarm.

**Sixth Trumpet - Army of 200 Million (Rev 9:13-19)**
"The number of the mounted troops was twice ten thousand times ten thousand." Modern militaries. Standing armies of unprecedented size. Mechanized divisions. The capacity for industrial-scale violence.

**Seventh Trumpet - Kingdom Proclaimed (Rev 11:15)**
"The kingdom of the world has become the kingdom of our Lord and of his Messiah, and he will reign for ever and ever." Global communications. The internet. The possibility of unified awareness. The proclamation reaching every ear simultaneously.
